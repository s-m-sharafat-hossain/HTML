Data Analyst

Role: Data analysts play a crucial role in interpreting complex data sets to help organizations make informed decisions. They utilize statistical tools and software to analyze data trends, patterns, and insights.

Responsibilities: Data analysts responsibilities include data collection, cleaning, and visualization, as well as presenting findings to stakeholders. They may also work on data modeling and machine learning projects.Data analysts often work with large datasets, requiring strong analytical skills and proficiency in programming languages such as Python or R.
